About

MindRX is a patient-facing app that educates young adults about FDA-approved mental health prescriptions and allows them to track their progress on prescribed medications. This app provides a safe space for users to learn about their conditions and treatment options, and to monitor important data such as intake, duration, and progress of treatment. The app includes features like medication intake streaks and notification reminders to promote adherence. The goal is to help patients make informed decisions about their care and improve communication with their healthcare providers.
